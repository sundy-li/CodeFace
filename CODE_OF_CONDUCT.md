# Code of Conduct

## Our pledge

We pledge to make participation in CodeFace a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of background, identity, experience level, or viewpoint.

## Expected behavior

- Be respectful, constructive, and specific.
- Assume good intent while focusing discussion on technical facts.
- Respect privacy and do not publish another person's private information.
- Accept maintainers' decisions about project scope and safety boundaries.

Harassment, personal attacks, discriminatory language, threats, sexualized content, and sustained disruption are not acceptable.

## Enforcement

Report unacceptable behavior privately through the maintainer contact options on the GitHub repository. Maintainers may edit or remove content and temporarily or permanently restrict participation when necessary. Reports will be handled as confidentially as practical.

This policy is adapted from the principles of the [Contributor Covenant](https://www.contributor-covenant.org/).
